How to use Privacy Policy Assistant

1

Hover over privacy policy links when browsing

When you browse a website, simply hover over any privacy policy, privacy terms, or similar links. Privacy Policy Assistant will automatically detect these links and display a blue icon.

When you hover over a privacy policy link, a blue icon will appear
2

Click the blue icon to get a summary

When you see the blue icon, click it to request a summary of the privacy policy. The system will begin analyzing the privacy policy document.

Click the icon to start analyzing the privacy policy
3

View concise summary

A few seconds later, you will see a pop-up displaying a concise summary of the privacy policy. The summary is categorized by importance, marked with different colors to help you quickly identify important information.

Privacy policy summary displays key information by importance
4

Click summary item for detailed explanation

If you want to learn more about a specific summary item, simply click on it, and detailed explanation and original text will be displayed.

Click any summary item to view details and original text

Using the Chat Feature

Privacy Policy Assistant includes an interactive chat feature that allows you to ask specific questions about privacy policies and receive detailed explanations.

1

Access the Chat Feature

After viewing a privacy policy summary, look for the chat button in the top right corner of the summary window. Click this button to open the chat interface.

Click the chat button in the top right corner of the summary
2

Ask Specific Questions

Type your questions about the privacy policy in the chat input field. You can ask for clarification on specific terms, request explanations about data usage, or inquire about any privacy-related concerns.

Type your question and press Enter or click the send button
3

Receive Detailed Answers

The AI will analyze the privacy policy and provide a detailed, easy-to-understand answer to your question. The responses are tailored to the specific privacy policy you're reviewing.

Get personalized explanations about privacy policy details
Example Questions

Here are some useful questions you can ask:

- "How long does this company keep my personal data?"
- "Can I request deletion of my data?"
- "What personal information is shared with third parties?"
- "How does this policy compare to industry standards?"
- "Explain in simple terms what cookies they use"

Direct Item Inquiries

You can also click the "Detail Explanation" button on any summary item to automatically open the chat with a specific question about that item.

Tips and Tricks

Color Meaning

The colors in the summary represent importance:

- Red: Most important, need to pay special attention to the terms
- Orange: Important terms
- Yellow: Medium importance terms
- Light Green: Low importance terms
- Green: Very low importance terms

View Original

Each summary item provides a "View Original" option, allowing you to directly jump to the corresponding paragraph in the privacy policy for further reading.

Detail Explanation Feature

The "Detail Explanation" button appears when you click on any summary item. This powerful feature allows you to instantly start a focused chat conversation about that specific privacy policy point.

When you click this button:

- The chat window automatically opens
- A pre-formulated question about the selected item is created
- The AI immediately begins generating a detailed, plain-language explanation
- You can ask follow-up questions specifically about this privacy policy item

This is especially useful for complex privacy terms that require more context or clarification beyond the initial summary.

Turn off/on feature

You can turn on or off Privacy Policy Assistant at any time by clicking the extension icon and then switching the toggle.
